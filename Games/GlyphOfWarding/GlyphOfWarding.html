Glyph of Warding is a exiting game mixing tower defence and rougle-like gameplay. Find artefacts of a past long gone, collect runes, create glyphs and cast spells to clear more of the dungeon. Find out what dangers are lurking beneath the dephts.

Development

This game is a solo project, so far i have made everything from scratch myself with no intention of changing it yet. I am making this project as something to take seriously and take to the Norwegian Game Awards (2025) and show off my own skill as a game developer.
